What goes inside the pressure panel?

The full-width card sits below the relay grid. What should it contain alongside the chart?

1

Min / Max / Avg strip

Three stat tiles below the chart showing the min, max, and average PSI for the selected time range — same style as the Supply Summary on PressureAnalysis.

2

Threshold reference lines

Dashed horizontal lines on the chart at the high-pressure alert threshold (86% of maxPsi) and optionally a low-pressure line — so you can see at a glance when readings were in the danger zone.

3

Relay-on shading

Vertical shaded bands on the chart showing when relays were active, so you can correlate pressure changes with irrigation runs.

4

Live PSI badge in header

Show the current live PSI reading in the panel header (colour-coded green/amber/red) so you don't have to look back up at the gauge card.
